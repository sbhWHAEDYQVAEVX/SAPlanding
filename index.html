Why most efforts fail.

Failure is rarely a lack of intelligence or effort. It is usually an internal misalignment. When thoughts, beliefs, and actions move in different directions, progress becomes difficult to sustain.

  • Unclear or shifting goals
  • Inconsistent thinking patterns
  • Scattered effort
  • Hesitation and self-doubt

The Alignment Framework
